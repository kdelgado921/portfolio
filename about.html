Hi, there!

My name is Keilani Delgado and I am currently a 5th year student at UC Irvine pursuing a B.S. in Informatics with a specialization in HCI and a B.A. in Psychological Science.

My background in psychology fuels my passion for human-centered design. I love bringing psychological insights into my work, creating designs that can truly benefit users. But my work is only just beginning! I’m always eager to grow and develop new skills.

Storytelling is at the heart of what I do—whether it’s describing my design processes or exploring fanciful ideas in my creative writing.

Outside of design, I enjoy reading, writing and journaling, teaching myself new languages, and spending quality time with my family.
